About Me

Curious about who I am? Get to know me a bit more!

Born in South Italy and raised in North Italy. I always felt like I had an english soul so I permanently moved in UK in 2013. Later I found and married my soulmate who happened to be Pakistani (I know, crazy right?!). I then discovered K-Dramas and developed a proper obsession for anythin korean! My passions are food (both eating and cooking!), mangas / webtoons, and music.

I wrote my first like of html and css when I was thirteen years old. I've always liked creating UI for websites but I spent my teen years as a computer technician also using and preaching Linux and Open Source as a member of a Linux User Group association (Lugman).

Moving to UK and getting a job as a full stack developer made my focus shift once again. After a few years of back and front end development I understood that my vocation was for Front-End only. So I decided to change job but instead of moving to a front-end web apps oriented role I landed on a special opportunity and became a Front End Game Developer instead!
